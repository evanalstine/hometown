Visit Brier

Enjoy this small city 15 miles north of Seattle, WA in the Pacific Northwest

Brier is a small city in Washington State covering roughly 2 square miles. It is located 15 miles north of Seattle, in Snohomish County. It is home to about 6,300 residents.

Your guide

Erica Van Alstine

What I loved most about my childhood in Brier was living down the street from Brier Park, and that I could walk or ride my bike to the local library and Brier Store.

Brier is a quiet place, where the speed limit is only 25mph, has so many fun, community events and everyone is so proud to live there. It has ample space for recreation, including many parks, trails and a horse arena. You might even see residents riding horses down the street! Be sure to check out Brier's city page for more information on all the fun to be had in the city of Brier.
